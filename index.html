COLOR SAMPLES IN LEXUS

Starting with the newest Lexus creation, the Lexus UX offers 10 exterior color choices and 5 interior color options for the model year. We have been fortunate enough to already get in most of the colors for the new Lexus UX crossover, but there are still a few that we have yet to see in-person at Lexus Dominion. In front you’ll see the UX in New Yellow, Redline Red, Cadmium Blue, Ultra White, Caviar Black. So you can see every color available, we tracked down pictures of the UX in Nori Green Pearl and Autumn Shimmer. The inspirations behind each color chosen for the Lexus model line up are as unique as the final expressions themselves. Often highlighting the bold, sculpted character lines of the new models, the Lexus design goal is to elicit an emotion at first glance. The Lexus model line up features new and returning Lexus colors that elevate the artistry of each model’s unique design. Redesigned and restyled for the model year, the new Lexus RC coupe is stunning.
